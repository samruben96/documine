Recommendation: Trust Blue

Based on the PRD requirements for "trustworthy, professional, calming" and the insurance industry context, Trust Blue is the recommended theme. The deep blue primary builds confidence, while the teal secondary adds a modern, tech-forward feel without sacrificing professionalism.
