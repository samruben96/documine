# 6. Market Opportunities

## Opportunity 1: The Trust Gap

**The Problem:** Only 4% fully trust AI agents, but 90% of executives see AI as strategic priority.

**Your Opportunity:** Position as the "accuracy-first" solution that solves the trust problem with:
- Source citations
- Confidence scoring
- Human-in-the-loop verification
- Transparent reasoning

## Opportunity 2: Document Comparison Pain

**The Problem:** Policy comparison across carriers is "incredibly difficult" with subtle differences easily missed.

**Your Opportunity:** Purpose-built policy comparison that:
- Understands insurance-specific language
- Identifies coverage gaps and conflicts
- Compares endorsements and exclusions systematically
- Works across P&C, Life, Health, Commercial

## Opportunity 3: Email-Centric Workflows

**The Problem:** Agents receive submissions via email but must manually extract and rekey data.

**Your Opportunity:** Email integration that:
- Automatically processes attachments
- Extracts key information
- Routes to appropriate workflows
- Maintains conversation context

## Opportunity 4: Knowledge Preservation

**The Problem:** Experienced agents retiring, taking institutional knowledge with them.

**Your Opportunity:** Adaptive learning that:
- Captures agency-specific patterns
- Builds searchable knowledge base
- Learns from corrections and feedback
- Creates company-wide intelligence
