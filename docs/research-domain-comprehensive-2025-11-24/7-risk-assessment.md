# 7. Risk Assessment

## Technology Risks

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| AI hallucination in policy analysis | High | Medium | Multi-layer verification, human review |
| AMS integration complexity | Medium | High | Partner with major AMS vendors |
| Model accuracy below 95% | High | Medium | Insurance-specific training, continuous improvement |

## Market Risks

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| Agent resistance to AI tools | Medium | High | Focus on "invisible" integration, productivity gains |
| Competitive response from AMS vendors | High | Medium | Build proprietary data moats, unique capabilities |
| Regulatory changes on AI use | Medium | Low | Transparency features, audit trails |

## Adoption Risks

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| "Technology won't deliver" skepticism | Medium | High | Proof of accuracy, pilot programs, ROI demonstration |
| Skills gap in agencies | Medium | Medium | Simple UX, training resources, gradual rollout |
