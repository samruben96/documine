# 1. Research Objectives and Scope

## Research Objectives

1. **Understand Differentiators** — What makes document/policy analysis solutions stand out in this space?
2. **Evaluate Reliable Technology** — What AI/ML approaches are proven and trustworthy for insurance document analysis?
3. **Map Agent Document Needs** — What specific documents do independent agents handle and what are they looking for?
4. **Understand Agent Workflows** — How do independent agents actually work day-to-day? What are their pain points?

## Scope and Boundaries

- **Product:** AI-Powered Document Analysis & Policy Comparison Platform
- **Target Market:** Independent Insurance Agencies (USA)
- **Verticals:** Cross-vertical (P&C, Life, Health, Commercial)
- **Key Features:** Email integration, URL scraping, custom knowledge bases, adaptive learning, accuracy-first architecture
